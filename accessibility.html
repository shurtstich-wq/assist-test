About the Program

ASSIST's Accessibility program provides design services and modifications that allow seniors and individuals with disabilities to remain safely in their homes. We believe aging in place is a right, and that thoughtful design can make that possible for more people.

Modifications We Provide

Bathroom Safety

Grab bars, walk-in shower conversions, raised toilet seats, non-slip flooring, and accessible layouts that reduce fall risk in the highest-risk room of the home.

Stair & Ramp Access

Handrail installation, stair lifts, exterior ramps, and threshold modifications that make entering and moving through the home safer and easier.

Doorway & Hallway

Widening doorways for wheelchair or walker access, lever-style door handles, and lighting improvements that increase visibility and reduce trip hazards.

Who Qualifies

Our accessibility services are available to seniors and individuals with disabilities who own their homes and meet income eligibility requirements. Priority is given to those with the most urgent safety needs.
